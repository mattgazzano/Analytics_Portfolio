About Me / Get in Touch

Empowering Business Users - From the ground up

My data analytics career began working at a healthcare media company where I was hired as an Operations Analyst to coordinate field service on a project to update digital advertisements on tablets in doctors offices. With the digital side of our business just emerging, I had to identify key areas of our operational process that required stronger data collection to produce more visibility for users and develop ways to improve upon them.

Through working in a startup environment, I became self taught on most analytics concepts in the context of our business. Starting as solely an Excel user, I learned the fundamentals of data manipulation, relationships, aggregation, and automation through VBA. However, when these skills capped out the growth I could achieve in my role, I challenged myself to explore relational databases and data visualization.

I developed a fascination with SQL to develop complex queries inside Google BigQuery that fed into visualization tools like Microsoft PowerBI and Tableau. Its a that point where work no longer felt like work! What started as simple weekly updates to my manager, became standardized & automated dashboards that are used across the organization. In May 2020, I was promoted to a Senior Business Intelligence Analyst role to help lead my department's reporting efforts and develop our data warehouse tool.

If my background resonates with you in how I can help your organization, please contact me.
