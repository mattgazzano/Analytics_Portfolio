Turning Data into Actionable Insights

Hi, my name is Matt Gazzano.

I'm a Senior Data Analyst in the information services industry where I help translate complex operational and financial datasets into actionable insights.

I help oversee the data collection, transformation, and presentation of company-wide dashboards that drive decision making for executive leadership.

I hold a BS in Business Administration from Ramapo College and an MS in Business Analytics (MSBA) at Montclair State University with a 4.0 GPA.

My Data Toolbox

I work with a diverse set of tools in order to accomplish the desired scope of my projects. Primarily, I use SQL & Data Warehouse tools (Google BigQuery), Advanced Excel (Formulas / Power Query / VBA), Python (Pandas, Numpy), and Visualization software (Power BI / Tableau)

Collect

Every successful analytics project starts with understanding the business needs and translating that into the right data model. That gets executed through setting up relevant points of data collection. I have extensive experience in CRM design (Zoho CRM), form design (Jot Form / Google Forms), and Project Management tools (Airtable) for integrating inputs into a data warehouse.

Transform




Once inputs are mapped, performing the necessary transformations to the raw data is the next step in order to make it useful for the organization. What are your KPIs? What standard reports or ad hoc analyses do you need to run? I will transform your data to unify metrics within your organization. My tools to accomplish this are SQL (Google BigQuery, MS Access, Oracle Application Express, MySQL) and Python.

Visualize




Lets turn your data into meaningful insights with the right visualizations. The best dashboards are ones that are intuitive to read and natively tell a story. These are my objectives when presenting data for any audience . I have experience with Microsoft Power BI, Tableau, and Google Data Studio.
